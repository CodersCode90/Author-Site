About the Book

Run Away, Blu! is a taut, psychological thriller about inherited trauma, buried secrets, and the razor-thin edge between self-doubt and survival. A story about a boy hunted by the past, and the terrifying possibility that it might be hiding inside him. A powerful coming-of-age story about breaking cycles, reclaiming identity, and finding hope where no one thought it could grow. It’s about the fire it takes to rebuild a life when the world keeps trying to burn it down. A searing tale of trauma, trust, and identity. A boy fighting to hold onto his soul in a world that keeps trying to tear it apart. It's a powerful story of resilience, redemption, and the search for identity. It’s about what happens when the world tries to break you—and you choose to rise anyway

This debut introduces readers to Blu’s story: raw, heartfelt, and unforgettable.

Synopsis

Anthony “Blu” Rogers was born into a name that carries blood. Branded as the son of two alleged murderers, he’s spent every day since drowning beneath the weight of a past he didn’t choose. The town whispers his name like a curse. His friends—Frank, Tyler, and Rosetta—once his lifeline, now treat him like something dangerous, something dirty. Even his own reflection feels like a stranger.

Abandoned. Neglected. Betrayed. With only the memory of his older brother to cling to, Blu is forced to survive in a world that has already decided who he is. Rumors call him demon spawn. Strangers cross the street to avoid him. And worse—there are people who would rather see him dead than hear him speak.

In the middle of it all stands Mr. Cross, a tough but principled cop—and the father of two of Blu’s ex-friends. He takes Blu in, not just out of duty, but something that almost resembles care. Still, the walls in that house echo with tension, and even protection comes with a cost.

Then Blu meets Morgan—unflinching, unapologetic, and completely uninterested in what anyone else thinks. She sees Blu clearly, and for the first time in a long time, so does he.

But even new beginnings come with shadows. The past doesn’t stay quiet. And as secrets surface, Blu must decide who he wants to be—not just in the eyes of others, but in his own. But Morgan has her own secrets—just like everyone else in Blu’s shattered world. And the closer they grow, the more dangerous the truth becomes.

As strange coincidences pile up and the lines between memory and reality start to unravel, Blu must face not just what others believe—but what he believes about himself. And when it all threatens to collapse, he’ll have to make a choice: Stand and fight—or run.
