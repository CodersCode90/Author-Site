About the Book

This book is about finding family in the most unexpected places. A group of teens—runaways, strays, and misfits—come together on a small farm and are welcomed into a home filled with love, care, and connection. Through laughter, struggles, and the bonds they form, they discover that family isn’t always who you’re born to—it’s who you choose to stand by, no matter what.

Synopsis

Fate unites a tiny group of teens on a small farm, some of whom are runaways. Each from a different place with different backgrounds yet somehow followed the same path to the same place.

The owners, Coop, Stephanie, and their twin children, Max and Stacey welcome Tori, Julius, Michelle, and the newest stray, Nathan into their lives, eventually forming an unbreakable familial bond.

Each one has a battle to fight and a story to tell. However, things begin to turn when Coop's past comes knocking at their door, which lands Nathan in the hospital.

The binds between lies and truths start to break. Drama, tension, trauma, and the war between love and hate will test these newly built bonds.

In the end, it’s a story of resilience, love, and the unbreakable ties that bind us all.
