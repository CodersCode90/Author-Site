About Michael Mangruen-Brown

Stories that linger long after the page is closed

Hello, I’m Michael

As a kid, my imagination was everything. It was my escape from reality where I could exist as anything or anyone. I created stories for years and only wished I could show others all of the crazy adventures I'd been on.

As I got older, the imagining never stopped but expanded. It became more clear to me what I wanted to do with my life: share my stories in whatever way I could. I have other ways to express myself from singing to writing song lyrics (where my love for writing truly began) and poetry, but it wasn't until my adult life that I wrote my first book "Run Away, Blu!".

My purpose in life had been realized and I couldn't be happier doing what I love. The journey was long and hard and I still have a long way to go with so much more to learn and experience.

I’ve been crafting stories since I can remember, exploring the grit and heart of coming-of-age journeys. My goal is to create narratives that resonate with readers, tackling love, loss, hope, and the unexpected twists of life.

My books include the Run Away, Blu! series, as well as upcoming titles exploring new characters and perspectives. Writing is my way of connecting with readers and sharing stories that stay with you.
